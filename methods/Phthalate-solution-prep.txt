Bis(2-ethylhexyl) phthalate (DEHP)

- listed on EPA's Toxics Release Inventory (TRI)
- DEHP is regulated under the Safe Drinking Water Act. The highest concentration allowed, the maximum contaminant level (MCL), is 0.006 mg/L.
- DEHP and DBP are listed as hazardous air pollutants under the Clean Air Act.
- BBP, DBP and DEHP are included in the first group of 67 chemicals to be screened as part of the Endocrine Disruptor Screening Program (EDSP).
